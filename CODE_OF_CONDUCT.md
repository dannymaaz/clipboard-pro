# Code of Conduct

Clipboard Pro follows the Contributor Covenant principles.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Help newcomers understand decisions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information.
- Spam or off-topic promotion.
- Repeated disruption of project work.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or restrict participation when behavior harms the project community.
